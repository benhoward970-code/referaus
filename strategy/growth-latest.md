## Strategy 1 - SIL Provider Acquisition Blitz (P0)

**The Opportunity:** SIL providers face mandatory registration by 1 July 2026. Many are unregistered and panicking. They need visibility NOW. ReferAus listing = social proof of compliance commitment.

**Core CTA:** "Get listed on ReferAus before July 2026 - show participants and auditors you are serious."

**Tactics:**
- Direct outreach to unregistered SIL providers via LinkedIn and NDIS provider directories
- Email sequence: 3-part nurture targeting SIL providers who have not registered
  - Email 1: "What mandatory registration means for SIL providers" (educational)
  - Email 2: "How a ReferAus listing demonstrates compliance commitment"
  - Email 3: Soft pitch - Free listing, upgrade to Verified Badge ($29/mo)
- Facebook Groups: NDIS Provider Support and similar - post helpful compliance threads
- Target geography: Start Hunter Region, expand to Newcastle and Sydney metro

**Conversion Path:**
1. Free listing (zero friction)
2. Verified Badge upsell ($29/mo) - trust signal during audits
3. Pro tier ($99/mo) - enhanced profile, analytics

**Metric:** 50 SIL providers listed by 30 April 2026
