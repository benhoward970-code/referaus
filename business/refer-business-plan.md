# Refer Business Plan
## referaus.com — Australia's NDIS Marketplace

**Mission:** Be the platform every NDIS provider and participant uses to find each other. A referral machine.

**What Refer IS:** A marketplace connecting NDIS participants with providers. Search, compare, read reviews, message directly, get connected.

**What Refer is NOT:** A rostering platform. A compliance tool. An internal management system. We don't run providers' businesses — we get them customers.

---

## The Problem

Finding the right NDIS provider is broken:
- The NDIS provider finder is clunky, outdated, and has zero reviews
- Participants rely on word-of-mouth, Facebook groups, and luck
- Providers struggle to get found by new participants
- No central trusted platform exists for NDIS provider discovery in Australia

## The Solution

Refer = the Seek/Yelp/Google Maps of NDIS.
- Providers list their services, areas, specialties
- Participants search, compare, read real reviews, message directly
- We're the trusted middle layer connecting both sides

## Go-To-Market: Provider-First Strategy

Providers come first. They pay. Participants follow the providers.

### Month 1: Seed the Directory
- **Manual outreach:** Ben + AI agents cold-email/call 200 Hunter Region providers
- **Offer:** "List free on Australia's new NDIS marketplace — launching March 2026"
- **Pitch:** "Participants are already searching for providers like you. Get found."
- **Target:** 50 providers listed with complete profiles
- **Key channels:** LinkedIn, NDIS provider Facebook groups, direct email
- Hook: July 2026 mandatory SIL registration — "Get ahead of the rush"

### Month 2: Build Trust + Reviews
- **Ask early providers** to invite their existing participants to leave reviews
- **Seed with 3-5 real reviews per provider** — this is the moat
- **Launch verified badges** — providers pay $29/mo, we check ABN + NDIS rego
- **Enable messaging** for Pro tier providers
- **Target:** 100 providers, first 10 Pro conversions

### Month 3: Participant Acquisition
- **SEO:** Provider profiles are public, Google-indexed with JSON-LD
- **Google Ads:** "NDIS providers near [suburb]" — low competition keywords
- **Content:** Blog posts — "How to find the right support worker in Newcastle"
- **Facebook groups:** Share Refer as a resource (not spam — genuinely helpful)
- **Target:** 500 participant searches/month, 50 enquiries sent

### Month 4-6: Expand + Monetise
- Open to Greater NSW
- Launch promoted listings (bid for top spot in search results)
- Launch ad spaces on category pages
- Hire first contractor if revenue supports it
- **Target:** $5K MRR

## Verified Badge System

**What it means:** "This provider has been verified by Refer"

**What we check:**
- Valid ABN (ABN lookup API — free)
- NDIS registration status (NDIS Commission public register)
- Active insurance (provider uploads certificate)
- Responds to enquiries within 48 hours (automated tracking)

**Display:** Blue checkmark on profile + search results. "Verified by Refer" badge.

**Cost:** $29/month add-on (or included in Premium tier)

**Why providers want it:** Trust. Participants prefer verified providers. Higher click-through rate in search.
